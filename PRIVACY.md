# SkillCheck Free — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 May 2026

## Summary

SkillCheck Free collects no data of any kind.

## What SkillCheck Free does

SkillCheck Free is a SKILL.md file installed locally into your AI agent's skill directory (Claude Code, Claude Desktop/Web, Cursor, or any other agentskills-compatible client). When you invoke it, your agent reads the SKILL.md file and applies its validation logic to your target skill file.

## What it does not do

- No network requests
- No telemetry, analytics, or usage tracking
- No data collection of any kind
- No third-party services
- No accounts, sign-ups, or logins

All processing happens locally inside your AI agent. SkillCheck Free has no servers and no backend.

## Your agent's privacy

This policy covers SkillCheck Free only. Your AI agent (Claude Code, Cursor, etc.) has its own privacy practices governed by its provider. Any skill file you ask your agent to validate is processed by that agent under its own terms.

## SkillCheck Pro

SkillCheck Pro at getskillcheck.com is a separate product with its own terms. This policy applies only to SkillCheck Free.
